> Are you ready to stay at the forefront of freedom technology innovation?

Freedom_Tech_Summit is happening right before BTC Prague 2026 - designed for developers, hackers, tech enthusiasts, and curious minds who want to dive deep into breakthrough ideas and cutting-edge technology.

For three years, we organized the legendary dev/hack/day and Cypherpunk Meetup - bringing together builders, hackers, and freedom advocates to celebrate technical excellence and the ethos of privacy and decentralization. Now we're combining both into a single, powerful experience.

We started with Bitcoin, continued with Lightning, then embraced Cashu and Nostr, and now we're exploring all freedom technologies including MeshCore and other decentralized systems that empower individuals.

If you're serious about development, hacking, and experimentation in the freedom tech space, this is where you need to be.
